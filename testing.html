Suggestions

Home Energy

  • Keep vents open and clean.
  • Insulate water pipes.
  • Wash clothes on cold.

Food Expenses

  • Don't be afraid to use coupons.
  • Buy what's on sale. Don't always resort to fast food!
  • Utilize store loyalty program.

Travel and Transportation

  • Roll your windows down instead of using AC.
  • Walk short distances instead of using ridesharing/taxi services.
  • Avoid toll rolls.

Waste Management

  • Buy recycled paper.
  • Have recycling bins readily available near all trash bins.
  • Reuse paper and aluminum products if possible.
