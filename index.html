One Step At A Time (OSAAT)

One Step At A Time (OSAAT) is a web application intended to help users understand contributing factors to their carbon footprint, like how often they drive or how much their home energy bills are.

As users begin to decrease their carbon footprint and notice decreases in their monthly savings, they will have the option to send the money to a local charity or organization that engages in philanthropic efforts.

Take the journey to decreasing your carbon footprint One Step At A Time!
